Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Whisper: Sentinel Zero ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Whisper: Sentinel Zero is designed with privacy first. The app does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII) without your consent.

  • Voice Input: Your voice commands are processed locally on your device. No recordings are sent to external servers without your permission.
  • Optional Cloud Mode: If you enable online features, some data (e.g., text queries) may be sent securely to external AI services for processing. This is always optional.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable technical data (such as app version or crash logs) to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information solely to:

  • Provide app functionality (voice recognition, AI responses).
  • Improve stability and performance.
  • Offer optional cloud-based features when explicitly enabled.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is only shared with trusted third-party services when you enable specific features, and always under secure encryption.

4. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All local processing stays on your device. When optional cloud requests are made, they are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

5. Your Choices

You control your data. You may:

  • Use the app entirely offline.
  • Clear app data at any time in your device settings.
  • Choose whether or not to enable online features.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the new effective date.
